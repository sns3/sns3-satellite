Steering commitee

The SNS3 steering commitee is an agreement between funding members of SNS3 software, with the objective of organizing the contributions.

The SNS3 steering committee aims are:

  • animate the community and particularly organise annual meeting with all its members,
  • coordinate the different jobs realised by the contributors,
  • define the technical orientations of SNS3(e.g. architecture choices, addition of new functionalities, maintenance choices, ...).
